Our Stories

Based on a true's one...

Once upon a time, there was a guy studying in college studying a course named commercial web application development. Because his teacher forced him to "swim on his own", plus his fellow group member refused to work, he was very resentful and angry...

"How to make a perfect website by yourself from front-end to back-end in just 2 months of studying only theory", Quoc said.

Then he got a pretty low score in this subject because he copied the ideas of those who did it before and only made a database on PhpMySQL.. .

Because he was still angry and he had an idea, but didn't know where to start, so he signed up for a javascript (witchCraft) course at MindX center.

Since then, The Witch Web was born with helping lost people like him create a website more easily by themselves even without any knowledge of web programming in mind.
